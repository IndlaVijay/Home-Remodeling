Our Story

Building Dreams Since 2005

Home Remodeling Services was founded by Michael Anderson with a simple mission: to provide homeowners with honest, high-quality remodeling services at fair prices. What started as a two-person operation in a small garage has grown into one of the region's most trusted remodeling companies.

Over the past 18 years, we've completed over 2,500 projects — from simple bathroom updates to complete whole-home transformations. Our growth has been fueled not by aggressive marketing, but by word-of-mouth referrals from satisfied homeowners.

Today, our team of 35+ licensed professionals continues to uphold the same values that built our reputation: integrity, craftsmanship, and an unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction.
