FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about our remodeling process, timelines, and policies.

During your free consultation, one of our senior designers will visit your home (or meet virtually) to discuss your vision, assess the current space, take measurements, and answer any questions. We'll discuss your budget range, timeline preferences, and design style. Within 48 hours, you'll receive a detailed written estimate with 3D renderings of the proposed design. There is absolutely no obligation — this consultation is completely free.
Our office is open Monday through Friday from 7:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and Saturdays from 8:00 AM to 2:00 PM. Construction crews typically work Monday through Friday, 7:30 AM to 5:00 PM. We can accommodate special scheduling needs for commercial clients or situations where work needs to happen outside normal hours. Consultations can also be scheduled for evenings upon request.
Timelines vary based on project scope: Bathroom remodels typically take 2–4 weeks. Kitchen remodels range from 4–8 weeks. Basement finishing usually takes 6–10 weeks. Exterior projects are 2–6 weeks depending on scope. We provide a detailed timeline during your consultation and maintain a 98% on-time completion rate. Your project manager will keep you updated daily on progress and any timeline adjustments.
Yes! We partner with several reputable financing companies to offer flexible payment plans. Options include 0% interest for 12 months, low-interest plans up to 84 months, and home equity options. We can help you find a plan that fits your budget during the consultation. Most of our clients are approved within 24 hours. We also accept cash, check, and major credit cards.

Copies of our certifications are available upon request.
