Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before starting your remodeling project.

Scheduling is easy! You can book online using our form above, call us at (555) 123-4567, or email info@homeremodelingservices.com. We offer free in-home consultations Monday through Saturday. During the visit, we'll discuss your project, take measurements, and provide a detailed estimate within 48 hours.
During your first visit, one of our certified project consultants will tour the space, discuss your vision and preferences, review material options, take precise measurements, and answer all your questions. We'll also discuss budget ranges and timeline expectations. There's absolutely no obligation — just helpful guidance.
Timelines vary by project scope: Bathroom remodels typically take 2-4 weeks, kitchens 4-8 weeks, basements 6-10 weeks, and exterior projects 2-6 weeks. We provide a detailed timeline during your consultation and keep you updated with weekly progress reports. Our on-time guarantee means we commit to deadlines.
We offer a comprehensive 5-year workmanship warranty on all projects. This covers any defects in installation, materials (subject to manufacturer warranties), and structural work. If anything isn't right, we'll fix it — no questions asked. Additionally, many of our material partners offer extended manufacturer warranties.
